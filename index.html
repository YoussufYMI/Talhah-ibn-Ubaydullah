Talhah ibn Ubaydullah (رضي الله عنه)

One of the 10 promised Paradise

One of the best quotes he said "I would rather die standing for the truth than live in comfort while abandoning it."

Here's a timeline of Talhah ibn Ubaydullah's (رضي الله عنه) life:
  • 594 CE - Born in Mecca into the Banu Taym clan of the Quraysh tribe.
  • Early Life - Grew up in a wealthy and respected family in Mecca
  • 610 CE - Accepted Islam at a young age after hearing about the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) from Abu Bakr (رضي الله عنه)
  • Early Persecution - Faced persecution from the Quraysh for embracing Islam
  • Migration to Abyssinia - Migrated to Abyssinia (modern-day Ethiopia) with other Muslims to escape persecution
  • Return to Mecca - Returned to Mecca after hearing false rumors that the Quraysh had accepted Islam
  • Migration to Medina - Migrated to Medina (Hijrah) with the early Muslim community in 622 CE
  • Brotherhood Pact - Formed a bond of brotherhood with Sa'id ibn Zayd (رضي الله عنه) in Medina
  • Battle of Badr (624 CE) - Participated in the first major battle of Islam, though he did not fight due to a mission assigned by the Prophet
  • Battle of Uhud (625 CE) - Played a heroic role, protecting the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) and sustaining severe injuries
  • Marriage - Married Umm Kulthum bint Abu Bakr (رضي الله عنها), strengthening his ties with Abu Bakr (رضي الله عنه)
  • Generosity - Known for his wealth and generosity, often helping the poor and supporting the Muslim community
  • Battle of the Trench (627 CE) - Participated in the defense of Medina against the Quraysh and their allies
  • Treaty of Hudaybiyyah (628 CE) - Supported the Prophet during the negotiations with the Quraysh
  • Conquest of Mecca (630 CE) - Took part in the peaceful conquest of Mecca
  • Battle of Hunayn (630 CE) - Fought bravely alongside the Prophet against the Hawazin tribe
  • Death of the Prophet (632 CE) - Grieved deeply after the passing of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him)
  • Election of Abu Bakr - Supported Abu Bakr (رضي الله عنه) as the first Caliph
  • Role in Early Caliphate - Advised and supported the early Caliphs, including Umar (رضي الله عنه) and Uthman (رضي الله عنه)
  • Battle of the Camel (656 CE) - Fought on the side of Aisha (رضي الله عنها) against Ali (رضي الله عنه) in a conflict over leadership. He was martyred during the battle at the age of 62

"Talhah ibn Ubaydullah (رضي الله عنه) is remembered for his bravery, loyalty, and sacrifices for Islam. His life serves as an inspiration for Muslims worldwide."
